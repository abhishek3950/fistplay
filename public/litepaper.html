**Rock, Paper, Scissors on Blockchain: A Social Gaming Platform** **Introduction** **Rock, Paper, Scissors** is a simple, timeless game that almost everyone knows. Our goal is to bring people together through a series of such easy-to-play games, allowing users to engage in lighthearted competition without a steep learning curve. In today’s world, where many people feel increasingly isolated, we believe that interactive gaming offers a powerful way to connect. The simplicity of **Rock, Paper, Scissors** allows anyone to join in, making it an ideal starting point for this social gaming platform. **Vision** The core idea of this platform is to offer **accessible social engagement through fun and simple games**. **Rock, Paper, Scissors** is just the beginning. Over time, we aim to expand this concept into a suite of casual games that foster connection and joy. The beauty of this platform lies in its ability to scale easily into multiple games, providing a variety of experiences that players can enjoy together, while maintaining the core principle: **bringing people together**. **Why Blockchain?** The decision to build this platform on blockchain technology stems from the inherent advantages that **blockchains offer** for incentivizing users and maintaining transparency. **Blockchain supercharges engagement** by allowing players to earn rewards for participation, ensuring fairness, and providing transparency. By utilizing a blockchain-based model, we can incentivize players with tokens, ensuring that they are rewarded for their time and engagement. **Why Build on Base?** We’ve chosen **Base**, an Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM)-compatible blockchain, for several key reasons: 1\. **Low Cost**: Base offers lower gas fees compared to many other blockchains, making it an ideal platform for frequent transactions like those in our game. 2\. **EVM Compatibility**: Being an EVM chain, Base benefits from the robust ecosystem that Ethereum developers are familiar with, ensuring easier integration and network effects. 3\. **Scalability**: Base’s infrastructure is built to scale with ease, making it an optimal choice for building a platform that will grow over time. **Tokenomics** At this stage, our **token model is designed to be simple**, rewarding players with tokens for winning matches. These tokens represent their engagement and success within the game. As we evolve, the model will become more sophisticated, incorporating elements such as: • **Early User Incentives**: Early adopters will be incentivized more significantly than later users, ensuring that those who help grow the platform are rewarded proportionately. • **Dynamic Reward Model**: The rewards players earn will evolve based on several factors, including frequency of play, winning streaks, and other game-specific metrics, creating a dynamic, engaging environment. **Roadmap** 1\. **Initial Launch**: Focus on perfecting **Rock, Paper, Scissors**, ensuring smooth gameplay, user onboarding, and the integration of token rewards. 2\. **Expansion to Other Games**: Introduce a broader catalog of simple, fun games that can be played for token rewards. 3\. **Incentivization Upgrades**: Develop a more **sophisticated token reward structure**, allowing for features like staking, governance participation, and more. **Conclusion** This platform is designed to be a **social gaming experience** that combines the **joy of casual games** with the **power of blockchain**.
